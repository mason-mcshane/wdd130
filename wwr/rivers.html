Located deep within the Frank Church Wilderness of No Return is the Salmon River. Many years ago, it was lovingly named the "River of No Return" by the locals. This was due to the fact that numerous tourists were drawn to the river's wild charm and found it difficult to depart.

The Green river meanders through canyons in central Utah, traversing some of the most remote terrain in existence. Its course leads it towards the powerful Colorado river and eventually to the vast sea. Towering cliffs of gray stone, captivating desert landscapes, and exhilarating rapids have all contributed to the renowned reputation of this particular route.
