Iron Man is the more offensive Character of this pair. He has a lot of quick barrage style attacks that come from his suit which can be lethal if he is to use them at the correct time. He also has the ability to fly, but for the sake of this matchup, not very far, high, or for very long periods of time.

Captian America is the more defensive Character of this pair, but he has extremely efficient shield usage. for the purposes of this website we are assuming that his shield can withstand any lightsaber strike and that he can summon it back at any time. He also has a good amount of raw strength that would be a soild contender to that of Anakins strong abilites.

Iron Man and Captain America fight well together when they take the time to plan out in advance. While they haven't trained together for as long as Anakin and Obi-Wan, they are certainly capable of putting up a good fight.
